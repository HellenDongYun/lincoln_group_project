Smooth recording workflow

  1. Confirm the participant Ask for a bib number, user ID, or email and make sure they appear on the registration list.
  2. Capture their finish Use the event clock to note the exact HH:MM:SS when they cross the line.
  3. Submit and spot-check Review the confirmation toast in the app and correct any typos immediately.

Pro tips for volunteers

  • Keep participant names private—use IDs or email when confirming identity.
  • Double-check the event selection before recording consecutive finishers.
  • Use the overwrite note for late corrections instead of deleting entries.
  • Flag anomalies for the race director if a participant has no registration.
